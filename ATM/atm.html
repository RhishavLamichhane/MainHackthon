ATM/Debit Card



In Nepal, ATM/debit cards are widely used for financial transactions such as withdrawing cash, making payments, and transferring funds between bank accounts. These cards are issued by various banks and financial institutions and can be used at ATMs, POS machines, and online platforms.

To obtain an ATM/debit card in Nepal, one must have an account with a bank or financial institution. The process of getting a card varies from one institution to another, but generally, one must fill out an application form and submit it along with necessary documents such as citizenship certificate, passport-size photographs, and proof of address.

Once the application is approved, the card is issued, and the account holder can activate it by setting up a PIN (Personal Identification Number). With the PIN, the user can withdraw cash from an ATM or make purchases at a POS machine. Some cards also come with additional features such as online transactions and international usage.

It is important to note that the use of ATM/debit cards in Nepal is subject to certain fees and charges such as ATM withdrawal fees, transaction fees, and annual fees. It is advisable to check with the issuing bank or financial institution regarding these charges before using the card for any transaction.
