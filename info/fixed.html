Fixed account



A fixed account, also known as a fixed deposit account, is a type of savings account offered by banks and other financial institutions. Fixed accounts can be an important tool for youngsters who are starting to learn about financial planning and investing. By providing a safe and secure way to save money and earn a guaranteed return, fixed accounts can help youngsters build a solid financial foundation for the future.
